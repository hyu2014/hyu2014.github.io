I got my Bachelor’s degree at Shandong University in 2018. I’m currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill. My advisor is Jiuzu Hong.

I’m interested in representation theory and algebraic geometry. My recent research mainly focuses on parahoric group schemes, affine Schubert varieties, and affine Demazure modules.

Preprint: Beilinson-Drinfeld Schubert varieties of parahoric group schemes and twisted global Demazure modules (joint with Jiuzu Hong). arXiv:2209.07347
